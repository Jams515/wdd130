Overview

Purpose

Are you interested in learning about young, talented artists? Learning about their visions and aspirations to enhance the world of art? I’m a computer science major that’s building a website that showcases those young and talented artists and their work, giving them a platform that allows others to see their work and potentially engage with those artists. It will give them a wider range of influence and opportunity that they might not get otherwise. I’m hoping to make the future of art more accessible than ever, elevating their platform into something that could help them launch their careers.

Audience

The audience for this website is for anyone looking to find original art, from primarily up and coming or unknown artists. It is a place to find all kinds of art, and all kinds of styles. The other audience this site will cater too is the artists themselves. The goal is to make a site that showcases art in a noncompetitive kind of way. All of the other art platforms have become essentially battlegrounds to try and grab an audience, and our goal is to mitigate that and make a safe easy space to showcase your art, one without the constant need to pump out as much art as you physically can.
