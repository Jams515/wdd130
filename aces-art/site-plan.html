Overview

Purpose

Are you interested in learning about young, talented artists? Learning about their visions and aspirations to enhance the world of art? I’m a computer science major that’s building a website that showcases those young and talented artists and their work, giving them a platform that allows others to see their work and potentially engage with those artists. It will give them a wider range of influence and opportunity that they might not get otherwise. I’m hoping to make the future of art more accessible than ever, elevating their platform into something that could help them launch their careers.

Audience

The audience for this website is for anyone looking to find original art, from primarily up and coming or unknown artists. It is a place to find all kinds of art, and all kinds of styles. The other audience this site will cater too is the artists themselves. The goal is to make a site that showcases art in a noncompetitive kind of way. All of the other art platforms have become essentially battlegrounds to try and grab an audience, and our goal is to mitigate that and make a safe easy space to showcase your art, one without the constant need to pump out as much art as you physically can.

Content

Home page

Audience- Our audience is anyone looking to learn more about the featured artist or their art. This website will be for a single artist and their art, to give them a place that they can direct others too so that they can learn more about them.
Purpose- The purpose of this website is a multifaceted affair. I am making it to learn to make websites and pass a class. I am also making this website for my sister-in-law so that she has somewhere where she can show off her art and let people know more about her. It will be a place where she can launch whatever platform she wants, that can link back to her social media and maybe even include a business email. For now, it will just be a place that will showcase her and her art, but eventually if she wanted it could even turn into an online store that she could use to sell art or accept commissions.
Future of the Site- If all goes well this site could become a place for any beginning artist to get a platform. For right now it’s just for my sister in law, but if this idea works out I could make a version that expands to include more artists and that will reach a broader audience.

About the Artist

Biography- Anais (Ace) has been drawing since she was 10 years old. Her style is heavily influenced by her beginning of drawing Anime and video game characters. Because of this, she mainly draws and paints humans. It slowly developed into being more realistic but still holds the simplicity of cartoon characters. Main subject of her art is Humans, the human figure is her muse. “I can draw anything if I stare at it long enough.” She is trying for a bright, smooth, elegant style. Her favorite art medium is watercolor, which she has been practicing for almost 2 years now. Kind of fitting because she’s obsessed with water; the element, what it represents/symbolism, its aesthetic and being in it. Swimming is her go to outdoor activity. She tried Prisma colored pencils, copic markers and acrylic paint before discovering watercolor is her favorite medium. It is smooth, fun to work with, and exciting. Water can be unpredictable. She loves the technique involved when using watercolor, how it can be light, soft and smooth, but also deep and chaotic. Born and raised in Houston, Texas. Moved to Idaho to attend University at BYU-I. Studied Animal Science for a year before switching to an Art major in 2021. She held off studying art because of the economic instability that comes from pursuing a career in Art. She knows she wants to be in a career she can be creative in, so for now, some kind of art degree will do. Her Instagram is @AceRianne. She follows many artists on Instagram, her favorites being Kelogsloops and Heikala. She has done a master copy of one of Heikala’s pieces.
