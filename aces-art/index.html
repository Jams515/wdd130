Ace's Art

Purpose-

The purpose of this website is a multifaceted affair. I am making it to learn to make websites and pass a class. I am also making this website for my sister-in-law so that she has somewhere where she can show off her art and let people know more about her. It will be a place where she can launch whatever platform she wants, that can link back to her social media and maybe even include a business email. For now, it will just be a place that will showcase her and her art, but eventually if she wanted it could even turn into an online store that she could use to sell art or accept commissions.
