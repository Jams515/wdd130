About the Artist

Influence

Anais (Ace) has been drawing since she was 10 years old. Her style is heavily influenced by her beginning of drawing Anime and video game characters. Because of this, she mainly draws and paints humans. It slowly developed into being more realistic but still holds the simplicity of cartoon characters. Main subject of her art is Humans, the human figure is her muse. “I can draw anything if I stare at it long enough.” She is trying for a bright, smooth, elegant style.

Medium

Her favorite art medium is watercolor, which she has been practicing for almost 2 years now. Kind of fitting because she’s obsessed with water; the element, what it represents/symbolism, its aesthetic and being in it. Swimming is her go to outdoor activity. She tried Prisma colored pencils, copic markers and acrylic paint before discovering watercolor is her favorite medium. It is smooth, fun to work with, and exciting. Water can be unpredictable. She loves the technique involved when using watercolor, how it can be light, soft and smooth, but also deep and chaotic.

Personal Life

Born and raised in Houston, Texas. Moved to Idaho to attend University at BYU-I. Studied Animal Science for a year before switching to an Art major in 2021. She held off studying art because of the economic instability that comes from pursuing a career in Art. She knows she wants to be in a career she can be creative in, so for now, some kind of art degree will do. Her Instagram is @AceRianne. She follows many artists on Instagram, her favorites being Kelogsloops and Heikala. She has done a master copy of one of Heikala’s pieces.
